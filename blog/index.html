Coronavirus

Hello to you all,

Firstly, I hope you, and your loved ones are all well, and bearing up in this terrible situation.

Sadly, due to Covid-19, I am currently unable to work at the Kibworth Medical Centre, however this is a temporary stop, and I am hoping to be back up and running in no time.

This does not mean I am not here to help you!

I am aware that many of my patients live alone, so if you would like a friendly phone call, let me know, and also to check you are feeling well in yourself. I care about you all, and I am still able to help you, just in slightly different ways at the moment.

Please remember to keep active and mobile. Movement is medicine for your body! Do your exercises, and if you have any problems, please just get in touch. I am still able to provide advice, support, and prescribe suitable exercises, so if you tweak something gardening, give me a call. (All of those DIY jobs are getting done now!) Avoid the temptation to sit all day, please, keep active. I have uploaded some of my Pilates videos onto YouTube, please email me for the link as this also lets me screen you so I know you are safe to do them.

I am regularly updating social media with some top tips for keeping safe whilst working from home, getting those ergonomics right is very important, please feel free to check them out on Facebook and Instagram.

This is a truly difficult time for us all, in so many different ways. Keep safe, keep well, and keep in touch.

Emily
