Osteopathy & Pilates

How can we help you?

We offer Osteopathic treatments to restore your body's musculoskeletal system to a state of balance to improve the way it functions.

These treatments are best combined with activity to maintain that balance once it's been restored so we also offer Pilates sessions to do exactly that.

This approach of restorative treatment and maintenance activity provides you with a great combination to support your wellbeing.

What is Osteopathy?

Osteopathic treatment focuses on the musculoskeletal system. All treatments are based on a carefully derived diagnosis which is in turn based on a patient's case history and examination.

These treatments use a combination of stretching, manipulation and massage to relieve muscular tension and improve blood supply and joint mobility.
