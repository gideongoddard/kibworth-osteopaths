Coronavirus

Hello to you all,

Firstly, I hope you, and your loved ones are all well, and bearing up in this terrible situation.

Sadly, due to Covid-19, I am currently unable to work at the Kibworth Medical Centre, however this is a temporary stop, and I am hoping to be back up and running in no time.

This does not mean I am not here to help you!

I am aware that many of my patients live alone, so if you would like a friendly phone call, let me know, and also to check you are feeling well in yourself. I care about you all, and I am still able to help you, just in slightly different ways at the moment.

Please remember to keep active and mobile. Movement is medicine for your body! Do your exercises, and if you have any problems, please just get in touch. I am still able to provide advice, support, and prescribe suitable exercises, so if you tweak something gardening, give me a call. (All of those DIY jobs are getting done now!) Avoid the temptation to sit all day, please, keep active. I have uploaded some of my Pilates videos onto YouTube, please email me for the link as this also lets me screen you so I know you are safe to do them.

I am regularly updating social media with some top tips for keeping safe whilst working from home, getting those ergonomics right is very important, please feel free to check them out on Facebook and Instagram.

This is a truly difficult time for us all, in so many different ways. Keep safe, keep well, and keep in touch.

Emily

About

Home > About

Emily Coombes (B'Ost Registered Osteopath)

Hello, and thank you for visiting my website!

I qualified as an Osteopath in 2009 from the University College of Osteopathy (formerly the Brisitsh School of Osteopathy) and immediately began to work in Leicester in a well established practice, under experienced Osteopaths.

I worked there for 11 years, and learned invaluable skills, adding to those gained on my degree. In 2017 I decided it was time to spread my wings, so, I opened Kibworth Osteopaths at the Kibworth Medical Centre.

I chose to work in a GP surgery, as I wanted patients to feel they were in a safe environment, and I am also determined to get Osteopathy more widely known as a profession, as well increase public knowledge about Osteopaths and what we do.

Testimonials

Emily is a fabulous therapist. As a qualified therapist myself I'm picky about who I'll let work on me, and I have no hesitation in recommending Emily. 5 stars all the way!

I can honestly say I love my job, and no two days are ever the same, I remember in one day my youngest patient was 9 days old, and the oldest was 99 years old! It is a privilege when people say 'help', and I understand that this can take courage, and requires trust in me.

I enjoy working out what somebody has done injury-wise, and I am passionate about rehabilitation as well as injury prevention. This is why in 2019 I trained with HFE to become a Pilates instructor, and qualified in 2020 as a Pre/Post natal Pilates instructor.

I am blessed to be married to my best friend who is superman to me. In 2011 we began the greatest adventure, I had our son, closely followed by our daughter in 2012. Being a parent opened my eyes to a new world, which is when I trained in Cranial Osteopathy, as well as learning a huge amount about treating pregnant patients.

In my spare time, I am either outside with my family and dogs, walking or camping, or I am curled up with a book and my cat!
