Coronavirus

Hello and I hope you're well.

I wanted to let you all know that I am open, and able see patients. The only patients I cannot see are those who have a positive COVID-19 status. The surgery looks and feels a little different, as I have removed everything that doesn't need to be there, to ensure maximum cleanliness. I also look a little different in my PPE. I can assure you I am smiling under the mask and visor!

I want to reassure you that I have made the clinic as safe as possible. These are the procedures that are in place:

  • All patients are screened the day before their appointment with questions relating to COVID-19.
  • Patients are asked to wash their hands upon arrival to the clinic.
  • I am asking where possible that patients attend their appointments alone, unless the appointment is for a child or vulnerable adult, in which case I ask that only ONE chaperone attend.
  • I wear full PPE throughout the appointment (mask, visor, apron etc) which is changed for every patient.
  • Full and thorough hygiene measures are in place, including but not limited to:
    • Thorough cleaning of the treatment room and waiting areas with Chlor-Clean in between patients.
    • New disposable plinth covers (changed for every patient)
    • Waterproof and antibacterial pillow protectors (changed for every patient).
  • I have completed a full COVID-19 policy for the surgery which can be accessed

    here.

If you have any questions about Osteopathy or Pilates, please do not hesitate to contact me. I look forward to seeing you soon,
