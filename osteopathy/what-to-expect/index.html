What to expect

Home > Osteopathy > What to expect

Your first appointment

Initial appoints are usually 1 hour. A full medical history is taken and an examination will be carried out - so wearing gym clothing is ideal.

You'll get an explanation of what the injury is, why it has happened, and we'll discuss a treatment plan with you. Treatment plans usually consist of short, medium and long term goals, and these are unique to each patient.

During the initial appointment, treatment is usually provided using a combination of soft-tissue release work for tight muscles, tendons or ligaments, specific joint mobilisation techniques and much more.

Advice on self-help is provided to help keep the recovery process active (e.g. stretches, ice or heat, ergonomic advice).

After your inital treatment

Any subsequent treatments are 30 minutes, unless an injury is complex or there are multiple injury sites - then more time may be required.

Self-help advice is regularly updated to keep the recovery process moving forward as injury moves towards total recovery. Advice is given on what can be done to keep symptom reoccurrace risk at a minimum.

Informed patient consent is very important to me, and this is an on-going process. No treatment will ever be given without a through explanation of what can be done and how (the techniques I feel would be most safe and effective for you).
